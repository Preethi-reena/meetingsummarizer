How To Use

  1. Click "Start Free".
  2. Click on "Start Recording" to record your meetings.
  3. Once Meeting completed, click on "Stop recording".
  4. If you want hear the meeting recordings, then click on the play button.
  5. You can also download the audio.
  6. Click "Summarize".
  7. If there is error in audio, it will show a Error.
  8. If no error are found, then you can download the text by clicking "Download"
